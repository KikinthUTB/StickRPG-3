# Stick RPG 3 - Design Document

## Vision

Stick RPG 3 aims to capture the charm and addictive gameplay of the original Stick RPG series while introducing modern game design elements and expanded features.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. **Wake Up** - Start each day
2. **Work/Study** - Improve stats and earn money
3. **Socialize** - Build relationships
4. **Explore** - Discover new locations and opportunities
5. **Rest** - End the day and repeat

## Character Systems

### Stats
- **Strength** - Physical power, unlocks certain jobs and activities
- **Intelligence** - Mental capacity, affects learning and high-skill jobs
- **Charm** - Social ability, influences relationships and persuasion

### Progression
- Stats increase through specific activities (gym, study, social interactions)
- Money earned through jobs scales with relevant stats
- Unlockable content based on stat thresholds

## World Design

### Locations
- Residential areas
- Commercial districts
- Educational facilities
- Entertainment venues
- Work locations

### Time System
- Day/night cycle
- Different activities available at different times
- Scheduled events and opportunities

## Economy

### Jobs
- Entry-level positions (low stat requirements, low pay)
- Mid-tier careers (moderate requirements, decent pay)
- High-level professions (high requirements, excellent pay)

### Purchases
- Housing upgrades
- Vehicles
- Consumables (food, drinks)
- Stat-boosting items
- Collectibles

## Social Systems

### NPCs
- Unique characters with personalities
- Relationship meters
- Dialogue trees
- Quest givers

### Interactions
- Conversation options
- Gift giving
- Activity partners
- Romance options

## Technical Considerations

### Architecture
- Modular game systems
- Save/load functionality
- Event-driven design
- Scalable content structure

### Future Expansion
- Additional locations
- More jobs and activities
- Expanded story elements
- Mini-games
- Multiplayer features (potential)

## Design Principles

1. **Accessibility** - Easy to learn, difficult to master
2. **Player Choice** - Multiple paths to success
3. **Progression** - Constant feeling of advancement
4. **Replayability** - Different experiences on each playthrough
5. **Nostalgia** - Honor the original while innovating

## Success Metrics

- Player engagement (time played per session)
- Completion rates
- Stat diversity (players trying different builds)
- Community feedback
