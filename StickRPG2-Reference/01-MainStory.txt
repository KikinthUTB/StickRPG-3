# Stick RPG 2 - Main Story

## Overview
Stick RPG 2 follows the protagonist who is transported from their dimension into a 2D stick figure world. The main objective is to build a successful life while uncovering the mystery of how to return home or deciding to stay in this new dimension.

## Story Arc

### Beginning
- Player character is mysteriously transported to the 2D world
- Wakes up on a park bench with no money, no job, and no place to live
- Must adapt to survive in this new reality
- Initial goal: Find shelter, get a job, and build a life

### Early Game
- Player meets various NPCs who provide guidance
- Learn about the different islands and opportunities available
- Discover the existence of the mysterious "Director" character
- Begin to uncover clues about dimensional travel

### Mid Game
- Unlock access to new islands through stat requirements and story progression
- Meet Professor Ansel who provides information about dimensional science
- Discover the X-Gen Energy Drink and its mysterious properties
- Learn about the Corporation and its influence over the city

### Late Game
- Confront the Director and learn the truth about dimensional travel
- Make critical choices about returning home vs. staying
- Complete various side missions that flesh out the world's lore
- Achieve one of multiple endings based on player choices and stats

## Key Story Elements

### The Director
- Mysterious figure who seems to control aspects of the dimensional reality
- Final confrontation reveals deeper truths about the 2D world
- Connected to the Corporation and dimensional experiments

### Professor Ansel
- Scientist who studies dimensional travel
- Provides equipment and knowledge to the player
- Key ally in understanding how to navigate between dimensions

### The Corporation
- Powerful business entity controlling much of the city
- Connected to dimensional experiments and X-Gen Energy Drink
- Offers high-paying jobs but with questionable ethics

### Dimensional Travel
- Central mystery of the game
- X-Gen Energy Drink is somehow connected to dimensional properties
- Multiple theories and explanations provided throughout the game

## Endings

### Return Home Ending
- Requires specific stat thresholds and story completion
- Player finds a way back to the 3D dimension
- Leaves the 2D world behind

### Stay in 2D Ending
- Player decides to remain in the stick figure dimension
- Can achieve various levels of success (from homeless to CEO)
- Multiple variations based on wealth, relationships, and karma

### Ultimate Ending
- Requires maximum stats and completion of all major questlines
- Unlocks special content and achievements
- Player becomes a master of both dimensions

## Side Storylines

### The Mobster Connection
- Interact with organized crime elements
- Choose to work with or against the criminal underworld
- Affects karma and available opportunities

### The Casino Heist
- Optional major side quest
- Requires high stats and specific items
- Can lead to massive wealth or catastrophic failure

### Romance Storylines
- Multiple relationship options with different NPCs
- Each has their own mini-storyline and requirements
- Affects ending variations

### The Mystery of the Z Island
- Hidden area with special challenges
- Contains end-game content
- Reveals additional lore about the dimensional nature of the world

## Themes
- Identity and adaptation in a strange world
- Balance between ethics and success
- The nature of reality and dimensions
- Personal growth and self-improvement
- Choice and consequence

## Notes for Stick RPG 3
- Consider expanding the dimensional travel concept
- Build on the mystery element that drove player engagement
- Multiple endings based on player choices were well-received
- NPCs with deeper storylines add replay value
- Balance between sandbox freedom and narrative direction
