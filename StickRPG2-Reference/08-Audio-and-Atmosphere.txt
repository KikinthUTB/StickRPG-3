# Stick RPG 2 - Audio and Atmosphere

## The "Flash Game" Vibe
Stick RPG 2 is a product of the Golden Age of Flash Gaming. Its audio identity is defined by compressed, synthesized, and catchy loops.

## Soundtrack Style
*   **Composer:** Eric Cheng / Nano Uribe.
*   **Genre:** Chiptune-adjacent, Synth-Pop, Ambient Electronica.
*   **Key Tracks:**
    *   **"Home Sweet Home":** Relaxed, ambient, slightly melancholic. Plays in the apartment.
    *   **"Main Theme":** Upbeat, adventurous, high tempo.
    *   **"Night Club":** Thumping bass, techno beat.
    *   **"Combat / Street Fight":** Fast-paced, tense, rock-influenced.
*   **Function:** The music changes dynamically based on **Location** (Island 1 vs Island 4) and **Activity** (Fighting vs Sleeping).

## Sound Effects (SFX)
*   **UI Sounds:** High-pitched "Bleeps" and "Bloops" when hovering over menus. Very responsive.
*   **Combat:**
    *   **Punch:** Cartoonish "Whack" or "Thud".
    *   **Gunshot:** Loud, compressed "Bang".
    *   **Laser:** Sci-fi "Pew".
*   **Ambience:** City noise, car horns, chirping birds (rare).
