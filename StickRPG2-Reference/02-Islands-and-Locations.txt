# Stick RPG 2 - Islands and World Map

## Overview: Paper Thin City
The world is divided into 4 main islands, connected by bridges and transit. The world expands as you gain stats and wealth.

## First Island (The Slums / Starting Area)
**Vibe:** Gritty, crowded, tutorial area.
**Key Locations:**
*   **The Basement:** Your starting home (Free).
*   **McSticks:** Fast food job and food source (Burgers).
*   **Wally! Mart:** General store (Furniture, Quest items like Painkillers/Self-Help Book).
*   **The Gym (Jim's Gym):** Basic strength training ($150 membership).
*   **University of Stick:** Intelligence training, Professor Ansel's location.
*   **Police Station:** Jobs, Bounties, Jail.
*   **Main Streets:** Where random fights and drug deals happen.
*   **Sewer Entrances:** Access to the underground (Mutants, Rat King).

## Second Island (Residential / Suburbs)
**Vibe:** A bit cleaner, middle-class.
**Locations:**
*   **Residential Homes:** Purchase better housing.
*   **Construction Site:** Home of the "Mike" construction worker quest line.
*   **The Club/Bar:** Nightlife, gambling access, Broski arm wrestling.
*   **Gun Store:** Buy Pistols, Shotguns, Ammo.
*   **Pawn Shop:** Sell items.

## Third Island (Business / Industrial)
**Vibe:** Urban, high-density, expensive.
**Locations:**
*   **Office Buildings:** High-level "White Collar" jobs.
*   **Casino:** Blackjack, Slots, Roulette. (Good for making/losing fortune).
*   **StickBucks:** Coffee shop (Energy items).
*   **Hospital:** Plastic Surgery (Change appearance), Health restores.
*   **Bank:** Loans and deposits (Important for earning interest).

## Fourth Island (The Future / Elite)
**Vibe:** Sci-Fi, Wealthy, End-Game.
**Locations:**
*   **Castle / Fortress:** Home of the high-end Government/Director content.
*   **Bakery:** Sells **Pecan Pie** (+Stat boost) and **Fruitcakes** (Instant Karma modification).
*   **Alien Crash Site:** Source of Alien technology/weapons.
*   **Luxury Housing:** The most expensive properties.

## Secret / Special Locations
*   **The Sewers:** Underground maze connecting islands. Contains "Sewer People" and rare items.
*   **Z-Island (Secret):** Often rumored, reached via glitches or cheats in base game, or specific modded versions.
*   **The Ocean:** If you swim too far, you drown or find Easter eggs depending on version.

## Traversal Options
*   **Walking:** Slow.
*   **Skateboard:** Faster, cheap.
*   **Scooter/Motorcycle:** Good speed.
*   **Sports Car:** Fast, status symbol.
*   **Hoverboard:** The ultimate vehicle. Obtained by giving all 4 Trophemon birds to the Activists. Hovers over terrain.

## Stick RPG 3 Design Notes
*   **Progression:** The island unlock system works well as a visual representation of progress (Rags -> Riches).
*   **Density:** Stick RPG 2 felt dense but small. SR3 should aim for fewer "dead zones" but maybe larger explorable interiors.
