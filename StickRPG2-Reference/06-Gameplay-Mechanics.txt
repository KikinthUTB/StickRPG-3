# Stick RPG 2 - Gameplay Mechanics

## Core Stat System

### Three Primary Stats

#### Strength
- **Description**: Physical power and athletic ability
- **Maximum**: 999
- **Training Methods**:
  - Gym workout sessions (+5-10 per session)
  - Construction work (+2-5 per day)
  - Fighting (+1-3 per fight)
  - Physical jobs (+1-2 passive gain)
  - Protein shakes (temporary boost)
  - Strength training manual (one-time +25)
- **Unlocks**:
  - Physical jobs (construction, bouncer, athlete)
  - Combat effectiveness
  - Intimidation options
  - Heavy lifting puzzles
  - Certain locations and activities
- **Effects**:
  - Damage in combat
  - Job performance
  - Physical challenges
  - Carrying capacity

#### Intelligence
- **Description**: Mental capacity and learning ability
- **Maximum**: 999
- **Training Methods**:
  - University classes (+10-15 per session)
  - Library study (+3-7 per session)
  - Reading books (+5-10)
  - Computer work (+1-2 passive)
  - Puzzles and challenges (+1-5)
  - Speed reading course (one-time +25)
- **Unlocks**:
  - Academic and professional jobs
  - Hacking abilities
  - Complex dialogue options
  - Research access
  - Strategic mini-games
- **Effects**:
  - Job availability and pay
  - Learning speed
  - Puzzle solving
  - Stock market success
  - Conversation options

#### Charm
- **Description**: Social ability and attractiveness
- **Maximum**: 999
- **Training Methods**:
  - Socializing at bars/clubs (+3-8 per session)
  - Conversations with NPCs (+1-3)
  - Dating (+5-10 per successful date)
  - Fashion/appearance upgrades (passive boost)
  - Charisma guide (one-time +25)
- **Unlocks**:
  - Social jobs (sales, entertainment)
  - Romance options
  - Persuasion choices
  - Better prices in stores
  - Exclusive social venues
- **Effects**:
  - NPC reactions
  - Relationship building
  - Negotiation success
  - Job opportunities
  - Social event access

### Balanced vs. Specialized Builds
- **Balanced**: All stats 300+ for versatility
- **Strength Build**: 800+ Strength, other stats lower
- **Intelligence Build**: 800+ Intelligence, other stats lower
- **Charm Build**: 800+ Charm, other stats lower
- **Hybrid Builds**: Two stats high, one moderate

## Time Management System

### Day/Night Cycle
- **Game Day Length**: ~30 minutes real-time (adjustable)
- **Time Periods**:
  - Early Morning (6 AM - 9 AM): Limited activities, some jobs
  - Morning (9 AM - 12 PM): Most businesses open, school, work
  - Afternoon (12 PM - 5 PM): Peak activity time
  - Evening (5 PM - 9 PM): Shops closing, entertainment opening
  - Night (9 PM - 2 AM): Nightlife, clubs, bars, some jobs
  - Late Night (2 AM - 6 AM): Few activities, dangerous areas

### Energy System
- **Maximum Energy**: 100
- **Energy Consumption**:
  - Working: -20 to -40 per shift
  - Training: -10 to -20 per session
  - Socializing: -5 to -15
  - Walking/Travel: -1 to -5
  - Fighting: -10 to -30
- **Energy Restoration**:
  - Sleeping: Full restore
  - Coffee: +20 energy
  - Energy drinks: +30 energy
  - X-Gen: +50 energy
  - Food: +10 to +30
  - Rest at home: +5 per hour

### Daily Schedule Planning
- **Optimal Strategy**: Balance work, training, and social activities
- **Example Day**:
  1. Morning: Work or training
  2. Afternoon: Second activity (work/train/socialize)
  3. Evening: Entertainment or dates
  4. Night: Optional third activity or rest
  5. Sleep to restore energy

## Money and Economy

### Income Sources
- **Jobs**: Primary income, $20 to $5,000/day
- **Investments**: Passive income, variable returns
- **Gambling**: High risk, variable
- **Crime**: Illegal, high risk/reward
- **Selling Items**: Liquidate assets
- **Mini-games**: Small amounts
- **Quest Rewards**: One-time payments

### Expenses
- **Housing Rent**: $0 to $10,000/day
- **Food/Drinks**: $3 to $100 per item
- **Transportation**: $0 to $2,000/day (insurance, gas)
- **Training**: $0 to $500/session
- **Entertainment**: $10 to $1,000
- **Items/Equipment**: $10 to $500,000
- **Services**: $15 to $500

### Wealth Progression
- **Broke**: $0 - $1,000 (Starting phase)
- **Getting By**: $1,000 - $10,000 (Early game)
- **Comfortable**: $10,000 - $50,000 (Mid game)
- **Wealthy**: $50,000 - $250,000 (Late game)
- **Rich**: $250,000 - $1,000,000 (End game)
- **Ultra Rich**: $1,000,000+ (Complete mastery)

## Karma System

### Karma Scale
- **Range**: -100 (Evil) to +100 (Good)
- **Neutral**: 0
- **Starting Value**: 0

### Gaining Positive Karma
- Helping NPCs (+5 to +20)
- Donating to charity (+1 to +10)
- Refusing illegal activities (+5)
- Saving people (+20 to +50)
- Legal jobs (+1 passive)
- Turning in criminals (+10 to +30)

### Gaining Negative Karma
- Stealing (-5 to -20)
- Violence against innocents (-10 to -30)
- Drug dealing (-5 to -15)
- Assassination (-50 to -100)
- Ignoring cries for help (-5)
- Working for mob (-5 to -20)

### Karma Effects
- **High Positive (+70 to +100)**:
  - Discounts at stores
  - Police favor
  - Hero reputation
  - Special "good" ending options
  - Cannot access criminal content
  
- **Moderate Positive (+30 to +69)**:
  - Generally well-liked
  - Minor discounts
  - Some legal advantages
  
- **Neutral (-29 to +29)**:
  - No special treatment
  - Access to most content
  - Flexible gameplay
  
- **Moderate Negative (-69 to -30)**:
  - Some NPCs hostile
  - Police suspicious
  - Criminal access opening
  
- **High Negative (-100 to -70)**:
  - Criminal reputation
  - Black market access
  - Special "evil" ending options
  - Legal paths limited
  - Higher police attention

## Combat System

### Combat Stats
- **Health**: Determined by strength and max health upgrades
- **Damage**: Based on strength + weapon
- **Defense**: Based on armor + strength
- **Speed**: Affects turn order and dodge

### Combat Types
- **Street Fights**: Random encounters, low stakes
- **Organized Fights**: Fight club, betting, rankings
- **Story Battles**: Boss fights, mission-related
- **Duels**: One-on-one challenges

### Weapons Impact
- Base damage + weapon damage = total damage
- Different weapons have different speeds
- Some weapons have special effects
- Ammunition management for guns

### Combat Strategy
- **Strength-based**: High damage, tank approach
- **Speed-based**: Quick attacks, dodging
- **Equipped-based**: Rely on weapons/armor
- **Avoidance**: Run from fights when outmatched

## Relationship System

### Relationship Levels
1. **Stranger** (0-19): Initial meeting
2. **Acquaintance** (20-39): Basic interaction
3. **Friend** (40-59): Regular interaction
4. **Close Friend** (60-79): Deep connection
5. **Best Friend/Lover** (80-100): Maximum relationship

### Building Relationships
- **Conversation**: +1 to +5 per interaction
- **Gifts**: +5 to +30 based on gift quality
- **Dates/Activities**: +10 to +20 per date
- **Quest Completion**: +20 to +50
- **Time Together**: +1 to +3 passive

### Relationship Benefits
- **Shop Owners**: Discounts, special items
- **Quest Givers**: Better rewards, exclusive quests
- **Romance Options**: Dating, relationship perks
- **Job Contacts**: Better positions, recommendations
- **Information Sources**: Tips, secrets, locations

### Relationship Decay
- **Ignoring NPC**: -1 per day
- **Standing Up**: -10
- **Rude Responses**: -5 to -15
- **Opposing Actions**: -10 to -30
- **Attacking/Theft**: -50 to -100

## Mini-Games and Activities

### Casino Games
- **Blackjack**: Card game, skill + luck
- **Poker**: Texas Hold'em, skill-based
- **Slots**: Pure luck, various jackpots
- **Roulette**: Luck-based, various bets

### Sports Activities
- **Basketball**: Shooting mini-game, strength + skill
- **Darts**: Precision game, charm + focus
- **Pool/Billiards**: Angle and power game
- **Fighting**: Combat mini-game

### Hacking
- **Puzzle-based**: Intelligence requirement
- **Rewards**: Money, information, access
- **Risk**: Can trigger alarms, security

### Racing
- **Vehicle races**: Speed competitions
- **Requirements**: Vehicle ownership
- **Rewards**: Money, reputation, vehicles

### Drinking Games
- **Bar challenges**: Various drinking contests
- **Effects**: Temporary stat changes
- **Social bonding**: Relationship building

## Transportation System

### Movement Speeds
- **Walking**: 1x base speed
- **Running**: 1.5x (limited by stamina)
- **Skateboard**: 2x
- **Bicycle**: 3x
- **Scooter**: 3.5x
- **Car**: 5-12x depending on vehicle
- **Bus/Taxi**: Instant to destination (with cost)
- **Helicopter**: Instant island-to-island

### Transportation Costs
- **Ownership**: One-time purchase
- **Maintenance**: Insurance, gas, repairs
- **Public Transit**: Per-trip fees
- **Convenience vs. Cost**: Trade-offs

## Progression Gates

### Stat Requirements
- Certain areas require minimum stats to enter
- Jobs have stat thresholds
- Story progression tied to stat levels

### Money Gates
- Bridge tolls between islands
- Purchase requirements for story items
- Housing progression

### Relationship Gates
- NPC relationships unlock content
- Romance options require relationship levels
- Faction allegiances open/close paths

### Story Gates
- Quest completion unlocks areas
- Story progression reveals new content
- Choices create branching paths

## Save System

### Save Locations
- **Bed/Home**: Primary save point
- **Auto-save**: Daily automatic saves
- **Quick save**: Optional feature
- **Multiple Slots**: Different playthroughs

### Save Data Includes
- Character stats
- Inventory and money
- Relationship levels
- Karma standing
- Story progress
- Time and day count
- Unlocked locations
- Quest status

### Negative Events

- Item breaking

- Random fights

- Time-specific events

## Notes for Stick RPG 3
- Three-stat system provides good balance and variety
- Time management adds strategic depth
- Energy system prevents grinding, encourages planning
- Karma system creates meaningful moral choices
- Combat should be skill-based, not just stat-based
- Relationships add personal connection to NPCs
- Mini-games provide variety and break from main loop
- Progressive unlocking keeps players engaged
- Save system should be flexible but not exploitable
- Random events add unpredictability and replay value

### Potential Improvements for Stick RPG 3
- **Four-stat system**: Add endurance/stamina stat
- **Skill trees**: Specialized abilities within stats
- **Crafting system**: Create items from components
- **More mini-games**: Variety in activities
- **Dynamic events**: More random encounters
- **Weather system**: Affects activities and mood
- **Reputation**: Separate from karma, faction-based
- **Achievements**: Reward unusual accomplishments
- **New Game+**: Carry over some progress
- **Difficulty settings**: Accessibility options
- **Multiplayer elements**: Competition, cooperation
- **Mod support**: Community content
