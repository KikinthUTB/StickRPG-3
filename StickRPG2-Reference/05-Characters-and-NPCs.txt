# Stick RPG 2 - Characters and NPCs

## Main Characters

### The Player Character
- **Role**: Protagonist
- **Background**: Transported from 3D dimension to 2D stick world
- **Customization**: Name, appearance choices
- **Development**: Player-driven stats and personality
- **Goal**: Survive, thrive, and potentially find way home

### The Director
- **Role**: Primary antagonist/mysterious figure
- **Location**: Corporate HQ - Executive Island (late game)
- **Importance**: Central to main story
- **Personality**: Mysterious, powerful, controlling
- **Story**: Controls aspects of dimensional reality
- **Interaction**: Final confrontation, reveals truths
- **Unlocks**: End-game content, multiple ending paths

### Professor Ansel
- **Role**: Key ally, scientist NPC
- **Location**: Research Laboratory - Second Island
- **Importance**: Story progression, dimensional research
- **Personality**: Eccentric, brilliant, helpful
- **Story**: Studies dimensional travel, helps player understand their situation
- **Interaction**: 
  - Provides quests
  - Gives scientific equipment
  - Explains lore
  - Optional romance path
- **Unlocks**: Lab access, special items, story missions

## Quest-Giving NPCs

### Old Man Jenkins
- **Role**: Mentor figure
- **Location**: Park, usually near bench
- **Personality**: Wise, mysterious, helpful
- **Quests**: 
  - Tutorial missions
  - Life advice quests
  - Hidden knowledge missions
- **Story**: Knows more than he lets on about dimensional travel
- **Rewards**: Wisdom, stat books, story clues

### Detective Morgan
- **Role**: Police contact
- **Location**: Police Station
- **Requirements**: Positive karma for most quests
- **Personality**: Professional, justice-oriented, tough
- **Quests**:
  - Crime investigations
  - Missing person cases
  - Corruption in the city
- **Story**: Investigating The Corporation
- **Rewards**: Money, gun license, police connections

### Marcus "The Fence"
- **Role**: Black market dealer
- **Location**: Back alleys, warehouse district
- **Requirements**: Negative karma or criminal connections
- **Personality**: Shady, opportunistic, connected
- **Quests**:

- **Rewards**: Illegal items, black market access, dirty money

## Antagonistic NPCs

### The Mob Boss
- **Role**: Criminal organization leader
- **Location**: Secret locations, contacted through underworld
- **Personality**: Ruthless, business-minded, powerful
- **Conflict**: Can work for or against player
- **Story**: Controls illegal operations, rival to Corporation
- **Interaction**: Offers criminal jobs or becomes enemy

### Corporate Goons
- **Role**: Corporation enforcers
- **Location**: Guarding corporate facilities
- **Personality**: Loyal, intimidating, following orders
- **Conflict**: Block player access to certain areas
- **Story**: Work for The Director
- **Combat**: Can be fought or avoided

### Rival Character (Chad)
- **Role**: Competitive rival
- **Location**: Various locations, mirrors player progress
- **Personality**: Arrogant, skilled, competitive
- **Conflict**: Competes for jobs, romantic interests, achievements
- **Story**: Alternative dimensional traveler
- **Interaction**: 
  - Challenges player to competitions
  - Tries to sabotage player
  - Can become ally in final missions

### Corrupt Cop Martinez
- **Role**: Crooked police officer
- **Location**: Police Station, back alleys
- **Personality**: Greedy, corrupt, dangerous
- **Conflict**: Demands bribes, threatens player
- **Story**: On Corporation payroll
- **Interaction**: Can expose or work with

## Random Encounter NPCs

### Street Thugs
- **Occurrence**: Random encounters in bad neighborhoods
- **Interaction**: Combat or intimidation
- **Rewards**: Money if defeated, items

### Panhandlers
- **Occurrence**: Common in city streets
- **Interaction**: Give money or ignore
- **Karma**: Affects karma system

### Street Performers
- **Occurrence**: Public areas
- **Interaction**: Watch performance, give tips
- **Benefits**: Entertainment, small stat boosts

### Lost Tourists
- **Occurrence**: Random encounters
- **Interaction**: Give directions for reward
- **Rewards**: Money, items, karma

## Notes for Stick RPG 3
- NPCs with depth and personality make the world feel alive
- Multiple romance options with different requirements = replayability
- Quest-givers should have their own stories and motivations
- Rival/antagonist characters add drama and competition
- Service NPCs with personality make routine tasks more engaging
- Random encounters keep exploration interesting
- Faction system through NPCs creates meaningful choices
- Consider adding:
  - More NPC interactions and dialogue trees
  - NPC schedules and routines
  - Friendship systems beyond romance
  - NPC conflicts that require player mediation
  - More consequence for NPC relationships
  - NPCs that remember player actions
  - Dynamic NPC reactions to player's reputation
  - Group activities with multiple NPCs
