# Stick RPG 2 - Items, Weapons, and Economy

## Currency
**$ (Dollars):** Used for everything. Earned via jobs, gambling, fighting, or investing.

## Consumables
### Food (Health & Energy)
*   **Grand Slam Burger:** +100 HP, +5 STR. Found at McSticks.
*   **Pizza:** +100 HP, +2 INT. Joe's Pizza.
*   **Fancy Coffee:** +15 HP, +1 Charm. StickBucks.
*   **Rad Bull:** +10 HP, +1 INT. "Energy Drink" parody.
*   **Pecan Pie:** **Major Item.** Costs $10,000 (Island 4). Gives +30 to ALL stats (STR, INT, CHM). The meta for end-game grinding.
*   **Angel's Fruitcake:** +50 Positive Karma.
*   **Devil's Fruitcake:** +50 Negative Karma.

### Quest Items
*   **Painkillers:** Used for Suicidal Goth Dude (Negative Karma).
*   **Self-Help Book:** Used for Suicidal Goth Dude (Positive Karma).
*   **Old Hotdog:** Give to Sewer Person.
*   **Trophemon (Birds):** Collectible. 4 total. Used to get Hoverboard.
*   **Crackbook Pro:** Laptop required for Hacking job.
*   **4D Items:** Hypercube, Klein Bottle, Mobius Strip (Main Story).

## Weapons
### Melee
*   **Fists:** Free.
*   **Old Baseball Bat:** $235. Basic weapon.
*   **Chainsaw:** Required for Lumberjack. Comes in CC sizes (45cc, 55cc, 70cc).
*   **Katana:** High damage, requires stats.
*   **Glowy Sword / Laser Sword:** Star Wars reference. High end.
    *   **Blue:** Positive Karma.
    *   **Red:** Negative Karma.
*   **Hall of Fame Bat:** Reward for Drug Dealer max rank.

### Ranged (Guns)
*   **Pistol / Glock:** Basic firearm.
*   **Shotgun:** High close-range damage.
*   **Combat Shotgun:** Faster fire rate.
*   **AK-47 / Machine Gun:** High DPS.
*   **Minigun (The King):** Massive damage. Reward for Government Official (King) rank.
*   **Railgun:** Futuristic high-damage weapon.
*   **Ray Gun:** Alien tech.

## Misc / Utility
*   **Alarm Clock:** Allows you to wake up earlier (more time in day).
*   **Coma-Snooze Bed:** Better sleep efficiency.
*   **Skateboard / Pro Skateboard:** Movement speed.
*   **Hoverboard:** Best vehicle (Flight/Speed).

## Economy Notes for SR3
*   **Inflation:** Stick RPG 2 economy breaks late game (Pecan Pie spam). SR3 needs better money sinks.
*   **Gambling:** The Casino was a notorious way to save-scum for infinite money. Needs balancing.
*   **Stat Buying:** The Pecan Pie mechanic essentially let you "buy" stats. This is good for end-game but trivializes training.
