## Item Locations Summary

### Where to Find Items
- **Convenience Store**: Food, drinks, basic supplies
- **Clothing Store**: Outfits, basic accessories
- **Gun Store**: Weapons, ammo (license required)
- **Electronics Store**: Computers, TVs, gadgets
- **Furniture Store**: Housing items, decorations
- **Sports Store**: Athletic gear, skateboards
- **Car Dealership**: Vehicles, insurance
- **Black Market**: Illegal items, rare goods (unlocks mid-game)
- **Beach Shops**: Swimwear, beach equipment (Second Island)
- **Luxury Stores**: High-end everything (Executive Island)

## Notes for Stick RPG 3

- Variety of items gives players choices in how to spend money
- Special items tied to story create meaningful rewards
- Vehicle progression speeds up gameplay as world expands
- Housing upgrades provide tangible goals
- Weapon variety could be expanded with modification system
- Consider adding crafting or customization mechanics
- Collectibles add replay value
- Illegal items add risk/reward decision making
