# Stick RPG 2 - Jobs, Careers, and Professions

## Job System Mechanics
*   **Shifts:** You must work a certain number of shifts to get a promotion (usually 3-10).
*   **Stats:** Every promotion requires higher stats (STR, INT, CHM).
*   **Rewards:** Promotions grant salary increases and often a unique **Signing Bonus Item** (Weapon, Vehicle, Hat).

## Civilian / Neutral Jobs
### 1. Fast Food (McSticks)
*   **Role:** Burger flipper.
*   **Entry:** No requirements.
*   **Reward:** Money + Burgers.

### 2. Skateboarding
*   **Role:** Skater.
*   **Entry:** Charm 40 + Own a Skateboard.
*   **Max Reward:** Pro Skateboard (Vehicle).

### 3. Graffiti Artist
*   **Role:** Street artist.
*   **Entry:** Charm 35.
*   **Max Reward:** Empty Paint Bucket.

### 4. Video Game Designer
*   **Role:** Developer.
*   **Entry:** INT 40, CHM 20.
*   **Max Reward:** **Masamune Katana** + High Salary ($175).

## Positive Karma Jobs
### 1. Police Officer
*   **Requirement:** **Positive Karma** + Strength.
*   **Path:** Meter Maid -> ... -> Chief of Police.
*   **Max Reward:** **Combat Shotgun** + Authority.
*   **Bonus:** You don't get arrested for carrying weapons as easily.

### 2. Light Knight (Director's Cut)
*   **Requirement:** Massive Stats (850 INT, 680 STR), Positive Karma, Glowy Sword.
*   **Role:** Jedi/Guardian figure.
*   **Max Pay:** $1,200/shift.
*   **Reward:** Blue Glowy Sword mastery.

### 3. Kitten Exterminator
*   Wait, actually this is **Negative Karma** (obviously).

## Negative Karma Jobs
### 1. Criminal / Gangster
*   **Entry:** Low Karma.
*   **Path:** Thug -> Mafia Boss options.

### 2. Drug Dealer
*   **Requirement:** Negative Karma, INT, STR, Coca.
*   **Path:** Street Peddler -> ... -> Cartel Leader.
*   **Max Reward:** **Hall of Fame Bat** + High Cash ($240/shift).
*   **Risk:** Can be arrested.

### 3. Pimp
*   **Requirement:** High Charm, Negative Karma.
*   **Max Reward:** **Pimp Hat** (Cosmetic/Stat boost).
*   **Pay:** High ($270).

### 4. Hacker
*   **Requirement:** **Crackbook Pro** (Laptop), High INT, Negative Karma.
*   **Max Reward:** "The Internet" (Item) + $275.

### 5. Dark Lord (Director's Cut)
*   **Requirement:** Massive Stats (680 STR, 850 INT), Negative Karma.
*   **Role:** Sith Lord parody.
*   **Max Pay:** $999/shift.
*   **Reward:** Red/Dark Laser Sword.

## Miscellaneous / Odd Jobs
*   **Lumberjack:** Requires Chainsaw. Minigame involves cutting wood. Reward: Bigger Chainsaws.
*   **Ice Cream Taster:** Requires +900 Karma. Pay: $10 (Joke job).
*   **Kitten Exterminator:** Requires -900 Karma. Pay: $5 (Joke job).
*   **Government Official:** (Director's Cut) Climbing to be "The King". Pays the highest legitimate salary ($2,420).

## Notes for Stick RPG 3
*   **Job Variety:** The mix of "Normal" (Burger flipper) and "Absurd" (Dark Lord, Kitten Exterminator) is key to the series' identity.
*   **Promotions:** The "Work X shifts to promote" loop is addictive but repetitive. SR3 could add mini-games to *perform* the job (like the Lumberjack one) rather than just a progress bar.
*   **Karma Lock:** Locking jobs behind Karma encourages replayability (Good run vs Evil run).
