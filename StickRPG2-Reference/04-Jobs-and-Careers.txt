# Stick RPG 2 - Jobs and Careers

## Job System Overview

Jobs in Stick RPG 2 require specific stat levels and provide varying pay rates. Higher-level jobs require higher stats but offer better compensation. Most jobs have a daily pay structure, with some offering performance bonuses.

## Entry-Level Jobs

### Fast Food Worker (McStick's)
- **Requirements**: None
- **Stat Focus**: None
- **Pay**: $20-40/day
- **Hours**: Morning, Afternoon, Evening shifts
- **Benefits**: 
  - No requirements, always available
  - Good starting point
  - Can work multiple shifts
- **Location**: Main Island - Commercial District
- **Notes**: Starting job for most players

### Convenience Store Cashier
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 10+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence
- **Pay**: $30-50/day
- **Hours**: Various shifts
- **Benefits**:
  - Employee discount on items
  - Slight intelligence gain
- **Location**: Main Island - Multiple locations
- **Notes**: Better than fast food, minimal requirements

### Janitor
- **Requirements**: Strength 20+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength
- **Pay**: $40-60/day
- **Hours**: Night shift primarily
- **Benefits**:
  - Strength training while working
  - Access to buildings after hours
- **Location**: Various buildings
- **Notes**: Physical job, off-hours work

### Paper Route
- **Requirements**: Bicycle or better transport
- **Stat Focus**: None
- **Pay**: $25-45/day
- **Hours**: Early morning
- **Benefits**:
  - Quick job
  - Freedom to do other activities
- **Location**: Residential areas
- **Notes**: Need transportation

### Street Performer
- **Requirements**: Charm 50+
- **Stat Focus**: Charm
- **Pay**: $30-80/day (tips vary)
- **Hours**: Flexible
- **Benefits**:
  - Charm increases while working
  - Variable income based on performance
- **Location**: Public areas, parks
- **Notes**: Income depends on player skill and charm level

## Mid-Level Jobs

### Retail Manager
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 100+, Charm 75+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence, Charm
- **Pay**: $100-150/day
- **Hours**: Full day shifts
- **Benefits**:
  - Staff discounts
  - Management experience
  - Small charm gains
- **Location**: Shopping Mall - Main Island
- **Notes**: Stepping stone to better jobs

### Construction Worker
- **Requirements**: Strength 150+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength
- **Pay**: $150-200/day
- **Hours**: Day shifts
- **Benefits**:
  - Significant strength gains
  - Overtime opportunities
  - Bonus for dangerous work
- **Location**: Construction Sites - Main and Industrial Islands
- **Notes**: Physical, well-paying

### Bartender
- **Requirements**: Charm 150+, Intelligence 75+
- **Stat Focus**: Charm
- **Pay**: $120-180/day + tips
- **Hours**: Evening/Night
- **Benefits**:
  - Free drinks
  - Social connections
  - Meet NPCs for quests
  - Charm increases
- **Location**: Bars and Clubs - Various islands
- **Notes**: Good social job

### Bank Teller
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 150+, Charm 100+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence, Charm
- **Pay**: $180-250/day
- **Hours**: Business hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Financial knowledge
  - Banking perks
  - Professional appearance required
- **Location**: Bank - Main Island
- **Notes**: Respectable position

### Personal Trainer
- **Requirements**: Strength 200+, Charm 150+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength, Charm
- **Pay**: $200-300/day
- **Hours**: Flexible
- **Benefits**:
  - Free gym access
  - Strength and charm gains
  - Train while working
- **Location**: Gym - Multiple islands
- **Notes**: For athletic builds

### Web Developer
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 200+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence
- **Pay**: $250-350/day
- **Hours**: Flexible, can work from home
- **Benefits**:
  - Work from home option
  - Computer skills improve
  - Tech industry entry
- **Location**: Tech companies or home (requires computer)
- **Notes**: Good for intelligence-focused players

### Real Estate Agent
- **Requirements**: Charm 200+, Intelligence 150+
- **Stat Focus**: Charm, Intelligence
- **Pay**: $200-400/day (commission-based)
- **Hours**: Flexible
- **Benefits**:
  - Housing discounts
  - High earning potential
  - Networking opportunities
- **Location**: Real Estate Office - Main Island
- **Notes**: Variable income based on sales

### Police Officer
- **Requirements**: Strength 200+, Intelligence 150+, Positive Karma
- **Stat Focus**: Strength, Intelligence
- **Pay**: $300-400/day
- **Hours**: Various shifts
- **Benefits**:
  - Gun license
  - Authority perks
  - Combat training
  - Cannot do illegal activities
- **Location**: Police Station - Main Island
- **Notes**: Blocks criminal path

## High-Level Jobs

### Accountant
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 300+, Charm 200+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence
- **Pay**: $400-600/day
- **Hours**: Business hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Financial insights
  - Stock market tips
  - Professional network
- **Location**: Accounting Firm - Main Island
- **Notes**: Stable high income

### Lawyer
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 400+, Charm 300+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence, Charm
- **Pay**: $600-900/day
- **Hours**: Long hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Legal immunity (minor offenses)
  - Court connections
  - High prestige
- **Location**: Law Firm - Main Island
- **Notes**: Requires business suit

### Stock Broker
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 450+, Charm 350+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence, Charm
- **Pay**: $800-1,500/day + bonuses
- **Hours**: Market hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Insider trading opportunities
  - Stock market advantages
  - Massive bonus potential
- **Location**: Stock Exchange - Executive Island
- **Notes**: Risky but lucrative

### Doctor
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 500+
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence
- **Pay**: $1,000-1,500/day
- **Hours**: Shifts (long hours)
- **Benefits**:
  - Free healthcare
  - Heal others
  - Respect and prestige
  - Medical knowledge
- **Location**: Hospital - Various islands
- **Notes**: Ultimate intelligence job

### Professional Athlete
- **Requirements**: Strength 500+, Charm 300+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength, Charm
- **Pay**: $1,200-2,000/day
- **Hours**: Training and games
- **Benefits**:
  - Celebrity status
  - Endorsement deals
  - Free sports gear
  - Fame
- **Location**: Sports Complex - Second Island
- **Notes**: Ultimate strength job

### Corporate Executive
- **Requirements**: All stats 400+, Suit required
- **Stat Focus**: All stats
- **Pay**: $1,500-2,500/day
- **Hours**: Business hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Company car
  - Expense account
  - Political power
  - Networking
- **Location**: Corporate HQ - Executive Island
- **Notes**: Balanced high-end job

### CEO
- **Requirements**: All stats 600+, $100,000 net worth
- **Stat Focus**: All stats
- **Pay**: $3,000-5,000/day + stock options
- **Hours**: Variable (mostly meetings)
- **Benefits**:
  - Massive income
  - Company ownership
  - Ultimate authority
  - Luxury perks
  - Penthouse access
- **Location**: Corporate HQ - Executive Island
- **Notes**: Highest legitimate job

## Special/Alternative Jobs

### Drug Dealer (Illegal)
- **Requirements**: Negative Karma, Street connections
- **Stat Focus**: Charm, Strength
- **Pay**: $200-1,000/day (highly variable)
- **Hours**: Flexible
- **Benefits**:
  - High risk, high reward
  - Black market access
  - Criminal connections
- **Location**: Streets, back alleys
- **Notes**: Risk of arrest, blocks legal paths

### Hitman (Illegal)
- **Requirements**: Strength 300+, Negative Karma, Criminal rep
- **Stat Focus**: Strength
- **Pay**: $500-3,000/contract
- **Hours**: Contract-based
- **Benefits**:
  - Massive pay per job
  - Weapon access
  - Fear respect
- **Location**: Underground contacts
- **Notes**: Very risky, moral implications

### Casino Dealer
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 250+, Charm 250+
- **Stat Focus**: Charm, Intelligence
- **Pay**: $400-700/day + tips
- **Hours**: Night shifts
- **Benefits**:
  - Gambling insights
  - Free casino access
  - High roller connections
- **Location**: Casino - Main Island
- **Notes**: Entertainment industry

### DJ/Musician
- **Requirements**: Charm 300+, Equipment
- **Stat Focus**: Charm
- **Pay**: $300-1,200/gig
- **Hours**: Evening/Night, weekend gigs
- **Benefits**:
  - Artistic expression
  - Fame potential
  - Club access
  - Creative work
- **Location**: Nightclubs, venues
- **Notes**: Gig-based, not daily

### Scientist/Researcher
- **Requirements**: Intelligence 400+, Professor Ansel connection
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence
- **Pay**: $800-1,200/day
- **Hours**: Lab hours
- **Benefits**:
  - Story progression
  - Special items access
  - Dimensional research
  - Unique missions
- **Location**: Research Lab - Second Island
- **Notes**: Story-related job

### Stunt Performer
- **Requirements**: Strength 350+, Charm 200+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength, Charm
- **Pay**: $600-1,000/stunt
- **Hours**: Project-based
- **Benefits**:
  - Excitement
  - Fame
  - Free healthcare (needed!)
- **Location**: Film Studio - Second Island
- **Notes**: Dangerous but fun

### Underground Fighter
- **Requirements**: Strength 250+
- **Stat Focus**: Strength
- **Pay**: $300-2,000/fight
- **Hours**: Fight nights
- **Benefits**:
  - Combat experience
  - Reputation
  - Prize money
  - Gambling opportunities
- **Location**: Fight Club - Underground
- **Notes**: Risky, can cause injuries

### Smuggler
- **Requirements**: Transportation, Criminal connections
- **Stat Focus**: Intelligence, Strength
- **Pay**: $500-2,500/run
- **Hours**: Night missions
- **Benefits**:
  - Travel between islands
  - Black market access
  - High risk profits
- **Location**: Docks, Ports
- **Notes**: Very illegal, high risk

## Passive Income Opportunities

### Stock Market Trading
- **Requirements**: Money to invest, Intelligence 200+
- **Income**: Highly variable, can be huge
- **Time**: Market hours, can be passive
- **Notes**: Risk-based returns

### Real Estate Investment
- **Requirements**: $10,000+ capital
- **Income**: $100-1,000/day per property
- **Time**: Fully passive after purchase
- **Notes**: Steady income stream

### Business Ownership
- **Requirements**: $50,000+ capital, relevant stats
- **Income**: $500-5,000/day
- **Time**: Can hire managers for passive income
- **Notes**: Various business types available

### Gambling (Casino)
- **Requirements**: Money, luck, skill
- **Income**: Highly variable
- **Time**: As much as you want
- **Notes**: Can lose everything

## Career Progression Paths

### Corporate Ladder
1. Retail worker → Manager → Accountant → Executive → CEO

### Academic Path
1. Library assistant → Teacher → Professor → Researcher → Scientist

### Criminal Path
1. Street dealer → Enforcer → Smuggler → Hitman → Crime Boss

### Athletic Path
1. Gym instructor → Personal Trainer → Pro Athlete → Stunt Performer

### Entertainment Path
1. Street performer → Bartender → DJ → Celebrity

### Professional Path
1. Bank Teller → Accountant → Stock Broker → Financial Advisor

## Notes for Stick RPG 3
- Job variety provides different playstyles
- Clear progression paths motivate stat building
- Mix of daily jobs and contract work adds variety
- Illegal jobs add risk/reward decision making
- Special jobs tied to story create meaningful progression
- Passive income allows late-game freedom
- Consider adding:
  - More gig economy jobs
  - Freelance opportunities
  - Entrepreneurship options
  - More crossover between paths
  - Job-specific storylines
  - Workplace relationships
  - Performance-based bonuses
  - Career achievements
